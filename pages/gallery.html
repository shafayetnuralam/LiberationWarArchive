Surrender Ceremony - December 16, 1971
Photo Details
  • Date: December 16, 1971
  • Location: Race Course Ground, Dhaka
  • Photographer: Unknown
  • Category: Historical Events
Description

This photograph captures the historic moment when Lt. Gen. A. A. K. Niazi, commander of Pakistani forces in East Pakistan, signed the Instrument of Surrender. The ceremony marked the end of the Liberation War and the birth of Bangladesh as an independent nation.
