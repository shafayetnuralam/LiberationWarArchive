About the Archive

The Liberation War Archive is dedicated to preserving and sharing the history of Bangladesh's struggle for independence.

Our mission is to collect, preserve, and make accessible the historical records of the 1971 Liberation War. Through documents, photographs, and oral histories, we aim to educate future generations about this pivotal moment in Bangladesh's history.
